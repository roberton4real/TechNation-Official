Our Story

John, Giselle, and Rayyan collaborated to create Footsteps, a mobile application designed to connect Canadian creatives with offline platforms for social impact. They identified a lack of artistic platforms in major Canadian cities and a decrease in civic engagement as significant issues. Their solution involves redefining what an art venue can be, allowing any establishment to become a hub for artists and active citizens. Users can set up profiles, and venues can mark themselves as available for artists to use. The app matches users based on shared social issues, fostering offline interactions that contribute to societal change. Future plans include integrating news features and augmented reality to further enhance user engagement. Unlike traditional data usage, Footsteps aims to use user data to bring people together and improve arts, society, and politics.
